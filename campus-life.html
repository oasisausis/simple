Two mistakes to avoid

🛏Leave the bedding at home

Mattresses, pillows, buckets, mugs and many room basics are available around the hostel area. Save luggage space for personal essentials.

🧺Do not hoard perishables

Humidity shortens food life. Keep snacks sealed and avoid storing damp packaging.
