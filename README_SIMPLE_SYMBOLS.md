# Simple symbol-first website

Designed for new students who may be unfamiliar with campus terms or long English text.

## Improvements
- Large, consistent symbols
- Short English labels
- Hindi helper labels on navigation, homepage cards, tabs and major headings
- Simple seven-item navigation
- Four-by-two homepage card grid on desktop
- Two columns on phones
- Floating emergency-help button
- Decorative formulas and clutter hidden
- Existing calculators, maps, tabs, search, reminders and call links retained
